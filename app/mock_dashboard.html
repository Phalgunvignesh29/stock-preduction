Stock Market Prediction Project

This is my project for predicting stock prices. I used deep learning (specifically LSTM and GRU models) along with sentiment analysis on news headlines to see if I can forecast where a stock is headed.

It's an academic research tool to analyze market trends based on historical data.

Current Model Results
